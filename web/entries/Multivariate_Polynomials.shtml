Multivariate Polynomials

Title: Multivariate Polynomials
Author: Alexander Maletzky and Fabian Immler
Submission date: 2016-07-06
Abstract: This formalization is concerned with multivariate polynomials over various coefficient structures (semirings, rings, fields). The polynomials are represented both abstractly, as coefficient functions with finite support, and concretely as association lists (thus allowing to perform actual computations). All ring-theoretic operations on polynomials, such as addition and multiplication, are defined and shown to satisfy the usual properties. Moreover, for polynomials whose corresponding type of power-products is ordered by a linear (term) ordering, various additional notions, such as leading power-product, leading coefficient etc., are introduced as well.
