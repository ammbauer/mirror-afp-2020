\begin{abstract}
An ambitious ethical theory ---Alan Gewirth's "Principle of Generic
Consistency"--- is encoded and analysed in Isabelle/HOL. 
Gewirth's theory has stirred much attention in philosophy and ethics and has been proposed as a
potential means to bound the impact of artificial general intelligence.
% We present an encoding of an ambitious ethical theory --Alan Gewirth's "Principle of Generic Consistency (PGC)"
% -- in Isabelle/HOL. 
% The PGC has stirred much attention in philosophy and ethics \cite{Beyleveld} and has been proposed as a
% potential means to bound the impact of artificial general intelligence (AGI) \cite{Kornai}.
% With our contribution we make a first, important step towards formally assessing the PGC and its potential applications in AI.
% Our formalisation utilises the shallow semantical embedding approach \cite{J23}
% and adapts a recent embedding of dyadic deontic logic in HOL \cite{BenzmuellerDDL,C71}.
\end{abstract}
